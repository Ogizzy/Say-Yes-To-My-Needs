Welcome to Say Yes To All My Needs Int’l Foundation.

SAY YES TO ALL MY NEEDS INT’L FOUNDATION is a Non-governmental Organization with aims and objective to empower Nigerian men, women and youth in a society home and abroad, Say Yes to All My Needs is plainly registered with Co-operate Affairs Commission (CAC) with registration No. CAC/IT/No 82241 and the foundation, grow to bring another child call Say Yes to My Needs Multi-purpose cooperative society union limited, which provided an opportunity for the entire members of the public to tap into the credits facility of the organization to meet their financial needs.

The objective of the company (Say Yes to My Needs Multi-purpose Co-operative society union Ltd) is to bridge the gap of financial needs of the general working class and individual business men and women. The company aims at satisfying customer’s needs by way of making goods and services affordable to them at all times and seasons on easy platform and reasonable affordable repayment terms and conditions.

The NGO (say yes to allmy needs international foundation) reserved the right and managerial discretion to assist any of the members, while say yes to all my needs co-operativesociety union Ltd is primarily set up to engage in micro-credit activities with different terms and conditions.
