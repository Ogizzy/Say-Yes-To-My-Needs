SAY YES TO MY NEEDS Implements projects directly and also works through non-governmental organizations and community based groups in Nigeria and beyond. Our strategy of working through partnership is intended to build institutional capacity of groups, supporting innovation and ensuring that the funding provided meets the needs of the target beneficiaries and is culturally relevant, Saying Yes to the needs of the less privilege in Nigeria and beyond.
