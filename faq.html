General Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)

  • Who is SAY YES TO MY NEEDS?

    SAY YES TO ALL MY NEEDS INT.L FOUNDATION is an Abuja based NGO with Aims and Objectives to address the issue of poverty in Nigeria and it’s environments.

  • What is our mission?

    Bridging the gap between the poor and the rich and developing human capacity”

  • What is our vision?

    Building a society where all citizens have access to quality Health care, education and equal opportunities to realize their potential.

  • Who are the financials?

    TOur financials are our International and home donors, and some of the financial institutions that we are into partnership with them like Access bank plc, we also have Individuals who always give us support to touched lives positively.

  • Who are the financial institutions that partner with us?

    TWe are in partnership with Access bank, GT bank, Heritage bank, and one of our big partner that care most for sponsorship is from Netherlands which we can’t mention them here in the public.

  • How many people that has benefited from this organization?

    We have empowered beyond mention, because some of our empowerments as a humanitarian organization we don’t put them in record, but we can only assured and proof to you that more than 3,500 people have received their empowerment one way or the other, visit our website: www.sayyestomyneeds.org and check under our past success.

  • Why is it that some members are yet to benefit from the organization?

    Some people who claims to be members of SYTOMN, we can tell you that they are not, because we notice sometimes 2018, people defraud some people that they will register them to SAY YES TO MY NEEDS but the registration was not properly done and handed over to us, and the ones that were submitted by this illegal registers, were not captured well for the kind of information’s we need from a member before getting empowerment.

  • Where and where can you find SAY YES TO MY NEEDS?

    We are all over the 36 states of the federation including FCT, Our membership base covers 774 Local Government in Nigeria, presently now, and we are developing branches to each and every council ward in Nigeria by appointing Coordinators.

  • Who are the targeted members you need to join this organization?

    Youth Empowerment both young and adult

  • Must every member of SAY YES TO MY NEEDS have Bank Account?

    Yes is very important because as the world is globally going digital, they encourage members to do that and presently now, we are doing cash empowerment into members account, because sometimes when you give a member at hand and you snap with them to make a publicity on what you have done so far, they do feel ashamed to appear.

  • How did SAY YES TO MY NEEDS do source money to empower her members?

    Through well meaningful Nigerians and International donors, our source also include a little token that members paid during registration etc, then we thereby make good use of it by multiplying in an investment that can bring R.I.O to enable us touched more lives.

  • How much SAY YES TO MY NEEDS is given members for empowerment?

    We cannot said this is the particular amount we are giving to members because their cases are of different categories some don’t even need cash but they rather need materially things.

  • Do I need to pay any body to register me as a member of SAY YES TO MY NEEDS?

    NO registration of Say Yes To My Needs is absolutely free whose ever ask you to pay money don’t pay and quickly contact our head office in Abuja with contact number 08025817715, but however if you are to visit any authorized business centre definitely at least you will pay for their data charges

  • Some members are still benefiting now?

    Yes every day by day we touch someone’s life in one way of the other.

  • Can we see the evidence of beneficiaries?

    Yes visit our website www.sayyesmyneeds.org and click on photo gallery

  • How many Nigerians SAY YES TO MY NEEDS want to register?

    We are provoke for the present situation of what Nigerians are passing through, therefore we want to enroll up to 37 million people that should be 1 million people per state and we divide them per Local government to Council ward, for them to come out of poverty and enjoy SYTOMN empowerment scheme frame work.

  • What age and gender need to be a member?

    From 13yrs upward you can register to become a member of say yes to my needs and both genders

    Generation upon Generation
